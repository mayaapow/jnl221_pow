Homework part one: answers

This data is from the BBC Visual and Data Journalism team and it uses a motion graphic of a flower to display how COVID-19 cases increase over time and how that affects the amount of people that die with the disease. It later has one that shows how the pandemic looks in each continent. There is also a legend that explains what the parts of the flowers and pedals in the graphics represent. The key message is that daily deaths around the world is higher than it has ever been, across different locations. The projects are effective especially in the section comparing the continents because the reader can easily differentiate the sizes in pedals. This is visceral and caters to emotions more because it uses simple visuals and vocabulary which makes it accessible to everyone depending on intellectual level. The flower design also has a simple and almost oddly relaxing style which makes it more emotional.
